I LOVE YOU A LOT MY PRINCESS!🎀🤍
In every breath, in every heartbeat’s call,
My love for you, steadfast through it all.
Through stormy nights and sunlit days,
In myriad, uncountable ways.

Your smile, a beacon in the darkest night,
Your touch, a comfort, pure delight.
Your eyes, a mirror to my soul,
In your presence, I am whole.

No mountain high, no ocean deep,
Can keep me from the vows I keep.
Through trials, joys, through rise and fall,
My love for you outshines them all.

In whispered winds and silent stars,
In every moment, near or far,
Unwavering, true, without end or pause,
Loving you is my heart’s cause.

For love like ours, so pure, so free,
Is boundless, timeless, meant to be.
A gift, a treasure, ever new,
My heart belongs, always, to you.
